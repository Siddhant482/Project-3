Utility Knife

Ultility knives are versatile tools designed for everyday tasks, from opening boxes to precision trimming in crafts or construction. They typically come in a few main types, fixed-blade ultility knives, known for strength and stability; retractable utility knives, which allow the blade to slide in and out for safety; and snap-off blade knives, which feature segmented blades you can snap snap-off to reveal a fresh, sharp edge. Prices vary depending on build quality and features, typically randing from $20-$40 or higher.
