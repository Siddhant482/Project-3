Typewriter

A typewriter is a mechanical or electromechanical deivce with an array of keys that, when pressed, cause an inked ribbon to strike the paper, printing a single character at a time to create legible, permanent documents. The main types of typewriters are categorized by size and mechanism. They are mostly bought these days by collectors and their price is highly variable, depending on the brand, condition and rarity. Most models sell between $75 - $300 but again, depending on the rarity and age, they can for couple thousand dollars.
