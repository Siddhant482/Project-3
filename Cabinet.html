Cabinet

A cabinet is piece of furniture chacharacterized by having shelves, drawers, or compartments, often enclosed by doors, and designed primarily for organized storage or display in various settings like kitchens, bathrooms, offices, or living rooms. The numerous types of cabinets are generally classified by location and style. The cost range is exceptionally broad; a single basic cabinet unit can start from under $100 - $300, while a full set of semi kitchen cabinets typically falls between $2600 and $10000.
